    Spaghetti Carbonara

    Ingredients:

    • Spaghetti
    • Eggs
    • Bacon
    • Parmesan Cheese
    • Black Pepper

    Steps:

    Step 1: Boil water in a large pot.

    Fill a large pot with water and bring it to a boil over high heat.

    Step 2: Cook the spaghetti.

    Add the spaghetti to the boiling water and cook according to the package instructions until al dente.

    Step 3: Prepare the bacon.

    In a separate pan, cook the bacon over medium heat until it is crispy. Once cooked, remove it from the pan and set it aside.

    Step 4: Beat the eggs and cheese.

    In a mixing bowl, beat the eggs and mix in the grated Parmesan cheese until well combined.

    Step 5: Combine the ingredients.

    Once the spaghetti is cooked, drain it and return it to the pot. Add the cooked bacon and egg mixture to the pot and toss everything together until evenly coated.

    Step 6: Serve and enjoy!

    Transfer the spaghetti carbonara to serving plates and garnish with freshly ground black pepper. Serve hot and enjoy!
